The Problem

Marketing execution still runs on human hours.

Today · the tool + agency stack

You're paying for output you still have to assemble.

  • Content tools draft copy — but a human still briefs, edits, schedules, and reports.
  • Agencies are slow and expensive; a campaign takes weeks and a retainer.
  • CRM and ad platforms don't talk; performance lives in 6 dashboards.
  • Lean teams cap growth at whatever 1–2 marketers can physically ship.
The gap

Nobody runs the work autonomously.

  • Generative tools assist tasks. They don't own outcomes.
  • The market needs a system that does the marketing, not one that helps you do it.
  • That requires agents with memory, tools, and judgment — newly possible in 2026.
